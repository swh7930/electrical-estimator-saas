What's New

Latest updates, improvements, and upcoming features.

Here you'll find product updates, feature launches, fixes, and other news for Electrical Estimator SaaS. Entries are organized chronologically, newest first.

Latest Release · October 2025

  • Faster estimating, consistent UI: Materials, DJE, Customers, and Assemblies use the same clean tables and headers for speed and clarity.
  • Simpler navigation: “Instructions” and “FAQs” are now in the main nav so new users can get help fast.
  • Company settings that flow into new work: Enter your company info, labor rate, overhead, margin, and tax once; new estimates pick up these defaults automatically.
  • Polish & stability: Dozens of refinements to readability, spacing, and reliability across the app.
Focus: usability & readiness for production launch.

September 2025 · Foundations

  • Database-powered libraries: Materials and DJE live in Postgres to keep data accurate for teams.
  • Estimator grid: Live Material & Labor totals as you type for quick checks while building scope.
  • Stable setup: Clean migrations and standardized environment configuration under the SaaS skeleton.
Focus: reliability, data consistency, and team readiness.

August 2025 · First Look

  • Modern base app: Flask SaaS skeleton with responsive layout and shared components.
  • Clear structure: Organized blueprints, models, services, templates, and static assets.
Focus: foundation for fast iteration.

Coming Soon · Public Roadmap

  • Proposal Generator: Branded proposals with scope of work, inclusions/exclusions, and terms—ready to send in minutes.
  • Change Order Tracking: Spin COs from estimate lines, track deltas and status, and keep a clean paper trail.
  • Project Management (lite): Tasks, due dates, files, and job notes tied back to the estimate.
  • BOM + RFQs: Vendor-ready bill of materials and one-click RFQ emails; log responses for quick comparisons.
  • Estimate Breakouts: Phases/areas/alternates with filtered subtotals and client-ready breakouts.
  • Nested Assemblies: Build multi-level assemblies from components; flatten for total hours and BOM.
  • Multi-user & Roles: Owner/admin/estimator permissions and feature gating.
  • Save / Load with Versions: Persist estimates with revision history for edits and what-changed reviews.
  • Export Packs: PDF/CSV/XLSX report bundles for clients and internal records.
Roadmap order and timing may change as we validate with early users.
