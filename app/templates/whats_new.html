What's New

Practical improvements that help you estimate faster, price accurately, and bid with confidence.

These updates are written for estimators—what changed, why it helps, and how it speeds up your workflow. Entries are chronological, newest first.

Latest Release · October 2025

  • Fewer clicks, same layout everywhere: Materials, DJE, Customers, and Assemblies now look and work the same—faster scanning and quicker edits while you build scope.
  • Help where you need it: “Instructions” and “FAQs” sit in the main nav so you can find answers without breaking your estimating flow.
  • Smarter defaults: Set company info, labor rate, overhead, margin, and tax once—new estimates pick up the right numbers automatically.
  • Smoother day-to-day use: Cleaned-up spacing, readability, and small fixes that keep you moving.
Focus: estimate faster with fewer surprises.

September 2025 · Foundations

  • Shared libraries that stay consistent: Materials and DJE live in one place so your team works from the same list.
  • Totals that keep you on track: Material and labor update as you type, making outliers easy to catch early.
  • Reliable foundation: Behind-the-scenes cleanup so things behave the same for everyone.
Focus: consistency and confidence as you build estimates.

August 2025 · First Look

  • Clean starting point: A responsive, organized app that makes future improvements quick and predictable.
  • Simple to navigate: Clear sections and shared components so you know where things live.
Focus: a solid base that won’t get in your way.

Coming Soon · Public Roadmap

  • Proposal Generator: Clear, branded proposals with inclusions/exclusions and terms—ready to send in minutes.
  • Change Order Tracking: Create COs from estimate lines, track status, and keep a clean paper trail.
  • BOM + RFQs: Vendor-ready bills of material and one-click RFQs; log quotes for fast comparisons.
  • Estimate Breakouts: Phases/areas/alternates with filtered subtotals so clients see exactly what’s included.
  • Nested Assemblies: Build multi-level assemblies and flatten them into hours and BOM for repeat scopes.
  • Multi-User Roles: Owner/admin/estimator permissions to keep edits controlled as your team grows.
  • Save / Load with Versions: Keep revision history for edits and quick “what changed” reviews.
  • Export Packs: PDF/CSV/XLSX bundles for clients and internal records.
Roadmap order and timing may change as we validate with early users.
