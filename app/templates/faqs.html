Materials, DJE & Assemblies

Materials: Individual items with cost, unit, labor unit, and unit of measure.
DJE (Direct Job Expense): Non-material costs (e.g., permits, equipment, travel).
Assemblies: Groupings of items/tasks used together, so you can add a bundle in one click.

Each item has a Labor Unit (hours per unit). Labor Adjustments apply multipliers for conditions (e.g., tight spaces, height, schedule). The app totals quantity × labor unit × any adjustment.

Yes. Use the Libraries pages to add or edit Materials, DJE items, and Assemblies, then pull them into estimates.

Troubleshooting

Check your email and password, reset if needed, and make sure you’re using a supported browser. If problems persist, contact support.

Create the estimate from the Estimates page (not by reusing a browser tab), and verify you’re not editing a clone. If something still looks off, save the estimate name, describe the steps you took, and email support so we can investigate.

Save the estimate first, then export. If the issue continues, share the estimate name, time of attempt, and browser with support.

Hard-refresh your browser, close other heavy tabs, and update to the latest version. If it continues, contact support with your browser and OS version.
